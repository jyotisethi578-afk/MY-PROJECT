About Us

International University provides quality education and focuses on skill development.

Rungta International University, Bhilai, is one of the leading educational institutions in Chhattisgarh. It aims to provide world-class education with a focus on innovation, research, and practical learning. The university offers various programs in Engineering, Management, Science, and Arts, helping students develop skills for global careers. With modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a student-friendly environment, Rungta promotes excellence in both academics and personal growth.
